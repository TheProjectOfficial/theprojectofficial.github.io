What is Aurora?

I still often get the question of what Aurora actually is, well now there is a whole page about it!

Aurora is essentially a centralized system or infrastructure that acts as a core structure for managing and linking data across multiple interconnected systems.

Over time, Aurora has grown in popularity and usage, making it central to more and more processes. This increase in usage brings about higher demands on the system’s resources, which can lead to a significant spike in energy consumption. If these demands are not managed effectively, the system could become unstable, potentially resulting in corruption of data or system errors.

If corruption were to occur, the solution might be to reset the entire system.
